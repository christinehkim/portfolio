About Myself

My name is Christine HaeWann Kim and I am currently a first-year student in the School of Architecture at Carnegie Mellon University. I recently graduated with honors from American Heritage School in Plantation, Florida. I am also involved in cheerleading and am a member of American Institute of Architecture Students.

Digital skills: Sketchup, AutoCAD, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, and Rhinocerous. Analog Skills: Drafting, Sketching, Rendering, Drawing, Oil Painting, Woodshed, and Power tools
